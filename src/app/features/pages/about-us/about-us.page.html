Marhaba! I am very happy that you have decided to learn the beautiful Arabic language! I was born in Beirut, Lebanon where I have spent all my youth and completed my pre-university studies in Arabic literature. Having had to leave Lebanon because of the war, I decided to spend the rest of my life relaying my passion for the Arabic language and culture to non-native speakers. I have completed my university studies at McGill University in Montreal, Canada. In 1999, I moved to Amsterdam where I founded my Arabic language institute "Master Arabic" achieving my dream of making a career of my passion. Since then, I have had the privilege of teaching Arabic as a foreign language to no less than 500 students and the mission goes on...

Discover "Master Arabic" at www.imasterarabic.com. Read the reviews under About us/Reviews. Drop me a note about your feedback or progress with the app. Would love to hear from you!
