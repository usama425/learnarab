For whom is this book by Master Arabic?

  • Everyone looking for an effective method in learning reading and writing Arabic. The student is not expected to have any previous knowledge of the Arabic alphabet.
  • The student who is familiar with the Arabic alphabet but would like to expand his/her knowledge and become more fluent in reading and writing.
